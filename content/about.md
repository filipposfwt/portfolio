**obal** is the artist name of **Vangelis Balis**, a graphic artist based in
Athens, Greece.

He works across prints, t-shirts, and ceramics, building a world run by satyrs —
horned, wide-eyed, and perpetually up to something. Drawn in a heavy black line
and washed in loud, unapologetic color, his characters pull from folk art and
comics in equal measure.

The medium changes — paper, fabric, clay — but the spirit stays the same: bold,
direct, and a little mischievous.

## What I make

- Prints & illustration
- T-shirts & apparel
- Ceramics
